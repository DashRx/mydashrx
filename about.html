About

Built around pharmacy workflows.

Our purpose

DashRx exists to help pharmacies extend care beyond the counter. When patients can’t get to the pharmacy, delivery becomes part of adherence, outcomes, and satisfaction. We make delivery dependable, trackable, and easy to coordinate.

Professional standards

Privacy-first delivery, clear handoffs, reliable communication.

  • Drivers trained to handle medications responsibly
  • Discreet delivery practices that respect patient privacy
  • Documentation options: notes, signature, photo when required

Pharmacy-friendly operations

Designed to reduce overhead and improve experience.

  • Clear status updates to reduce phone calls
  • Route options that fit your peak hours and volume
  • Simple onboarding: tell us your volume and service area
